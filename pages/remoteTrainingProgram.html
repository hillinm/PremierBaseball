Remote Programming Information


With the Kansas stay-at-home order going into effect on Tuesday March 24th, we are introducing remote weekly customized programming. This will be our only training option for the time being until the stay-at-home order is lifted.

Remote programming will consist of daily arm care mobility work and throwing program, as well as weekly video review.

For weekly video review, the athlete will need to provide video of throwing and submit to Premier and we will review and reply back with recorded voice-over instruction of the video, and then provide the next week’s programming based off the previous week’s video review and progress.

Cost: $50 a week. Payment option below.

Hitting remote programming also available!

Sign-up and contact form at bottom of the page.

Plyoballs and Bands Information


Plyoballs and bands will be essential to get the full benefits of our remote programming training.

Prices:

Plyoballs – $50 for a set, or available for individual purchase.

Bands – $25 per pair, or $60 for set of 3 resistances.
